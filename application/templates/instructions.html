How to use Project Riffbox.

Project Riffbox has a compilation of songs, hand picked for you. All you have to do is follow three easy steps!

Step One.

You can check the Index page to see what genres are currently available in Project Riffbox, along with some information on the making of such Project. More information here!

Step Two.

Project Riffbox will ask you for three genres of music that you find enjoyable, it will then generate a playlist of five songs for you to check out. You can start doing so here!
